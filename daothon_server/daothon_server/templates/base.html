About

A game jam is a hackathon for video games. A DAO jam is a hackathon for create a DAO(Decentralized Autonomous Organizations) in 7days. DAOs are groups of people united by trustless decision-making frameworks, usually built on the blockchain. They combine ideas about cooperativism with newly invented cryptography and networking technology.

How it work?

Each player will recieve 1000G at the beginning of the game. Then you can summon your own DAO and distribute your coin or join other DAO to earn more coins. Judgers and general audience can invest or sell your DAO's token at any time.
